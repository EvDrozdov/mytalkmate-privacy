Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 1, 2026

MyTalkMate is developed by DrozDEV as an independent iOS application. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user. We are committed to collecting the minimum data necessary to deliver the service.

Information We Collect

MyTalkMate does not collect personally identifiable information and has no backend servers. All data stays on your device unless explicitly described below.

The following data is stored locally on your device only via Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Practice session history (duration, score, accuracy)
  • Daily goals and streak progress
  • Profile settings (display name, target language, voice preference)
  • App preferences and notification settings

We have no access to this data. It is never transmitted to our servers.

Microphone & Speech Recognition

MyTalkMate requests microphone access to enable voice conversations during practice sessions. Your voice is processed by Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer, which may transmit audio to Apple's servers for transcription.

  • We do not record, store, or transmit your audio
  • Audio is processed in real-time and discarded after transcription
  • You can revoke microphone access at any time in iOS Settings

Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles speech data.

AI Conversations (OpenRouter API)

To generate AI responses, the text of your conversation turns is sent to OpenRouter API for processing. This includes:

  • The transcribed text of your spoken input
  • Conversation context for the current session
  • Selected language and difficulty level

We do not send: your name, email, device identifiers, or any personal information to OpenRouter.

Please review OpenRouter's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

Subscriptions & Payments

MyTalkMate offers optional Pro subscriptions. All purchases are processed exclusively through Apple App Store and managed via RevenueCat. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information.

  • Payment data is handled entirely by Apple
  • RevenueCat is used to verify subscription status only
  • No financial information is accessible to DrozDEV

See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for more details.

Data Storage & iCloud

All app data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. If you have iCloud Backup enabled in iOS Settings, this data may be included in your standard device backup — this is controlled entirely by Apple and iOS, not by us.

To delete all app data, simply uninstall MyTalkMate from your device.

Children's Privacy

MyTalkMate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app and data should be removed, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
