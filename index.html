Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly what we do — and don't do — with your data.

Last updated: May 20, 2026
01

Introduction

MyTalkMate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

By using MyTalkMate, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

02

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Voice recordings during active sessions (processed in real-time, not stored)
  • App usage data and performance metrics (anonymous)
  • Device information (OS version, device model) for compatibility
  • Account information if you create an account (email, display name)
03

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the conversation features of the app
  • Diagnose technical issues and ensure app stability
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send important service-related notifications (with your consent)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

04

Voice & Audio Data

MyTalkMate processes voice input to enable conversations. Audio is transmitted securely to our servers for real-time speech recognition and is not retained after your session ends.

We do not use your voice data to build personal profiles or for any purpose other than delivering the in-app experience.

05

Data Storage & Security

We take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Servers are hosted in secure, certified data centers
  • Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel
  • We regularly audit our security practices
06

Third-Party Services

MyTalkMate may use trusted third-party services (such as speech recognition APIs and analytics tools). These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

We do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs into the app.

07

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

08

Contact Us
